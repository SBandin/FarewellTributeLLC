# FarewellTribute.com

**Memorial Services | Automated Wake Video Creation**

FarewellTribute.com helps families create beautiful, personalized memorial tribute videos to celebrate the lives of their loved ones. Our platform makes it easy to compile photos, videos, prayers, and heartfelt messages into a professional memorial presentation for wake and funeral services.

## Features

- Upload intro photos and videos with optional jazz music
- Select from curated prayers with background music
- Collect tribute videos from family and friends via invite links
- Add a closing outro video
- Preview the full tribute before purchase
- Receive HD downloads delivered directly to your email
